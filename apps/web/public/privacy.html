Privacy Policy

Covaga ECAD Viewer · Last updated: July 16, 2026

Covaga ECAD Viewer is a viewer for EPLAN project exports (.epdz) and 3D parts (.e3d) that runs entirely on your device — as a web app, a desktop app or a mobile app.

Data we collect

None. The app has no server backend, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising and no tracking of any kind.

Your files

Projects and parts you open are parsed and rendered locally on your device. They are never uploaded, transmitted or shared. The app works fully offline once installed.

Local storage

To provide its features the app stores data only on your device: the list of recently opened files, linked folders and their index, thumbnails, your language/theme preferences, and the offline cache of the app itself (service worker). You can remove all of it at any time by clearing the app's site data or uninstalling the app.

Network access

The only network requests the app makes are to download its own updates and the bundled sample files from the host it is served from. No user data is ever sent.

Third parties

No data is shared with third parties, because no data is collected.

Contact

Questions or concerns: open an issue at github.com/covagashi/ecad-view or visit covaga.dev.
